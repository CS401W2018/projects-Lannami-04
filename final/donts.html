What to Avoid

When managing acne, there are certain habits and behaviors that can worsen breakouts. Avoid the following:

  • Touching Your Face: Hands carry bacteria that can transfer to your skin.
  • Popping Pimples: This can cause scarring and spread bacteria, worsening acne.
  • Using Harsh Products: Avoid over-drying products that strip natural oils from your skin.
  • Skipping Sunscreen: UV rays can worsen acne marks and damage your skin.
  • Sleeping with Makeup On: This clogs pores and causes breakouts.
