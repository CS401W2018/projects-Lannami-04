Final Project Proposal

By Lanna Eleazar

1. Title and Topic of My Site

Title: Flawlessly You: A Self-Care Guide for Tackling Acne

Topic: A website which focuses on supporting individuals with acne-prone skin by exploring its causes, offering effective solutions and habits, and inspiring confidence through self-love and self-care.

2. Why I chose this topic

I struggled with acne throughout my teenage years, and it deeply affected my confidence. During puberty, my face was covered with acne, which led to bullying and ridicule, leaving me feeling ashamed and unable to love myself. I want to create this website because, during my journey, I had no one to guide me, I had to face it alone and find solutions on my own. Through this platform, I hope to empower individuals with acne, reassuring them that they are not alone and that there are solutions to their challenges. Most importantly, I want to share the message of self-love: that even with acne, they are still beautiful. By providing tips, recommending products, and promoting healthy habits, I aim to guide others toward clearer skin and greater confidence.

3. Content I will have on the homepage and each of the five pages (one is a table)

My website will include a Homepage featuring an introduction to the site, along with motivational statements to inspire self-care, love, and confidence. This is where I can also put an introduction to causes of acne. The Lifestyle Habits page will provide practical tips such as changing pillowcases, washing the face, sleeping early, and managing stress to improve skin health. The Eating Diet page will highlight foods that are beneficial or harmful to acne-prone skin and offer guidance on maintaining a skin-friendly diet. The Skincare Products page, which will include a table, will compare different products based on factors like price, ingredients, and benefits for acne-prone skin. The Don'ts page will focus on habits to avoid, such as touching the face or popping pimples, which can aggravate acne. Finally, the Personalized Acne Solution page will include a form where users can input their skin details to receive tailored advice and recommendations.

4. Fields I wil put into my form, as well as the data that will go on my table.

Fields for my form: My form, titled "Personalized Acne Solution," will include fields to gather detailed user input for tailored recommendations. These fields include Name (text input) to personalize responses, Email (email input) for follow-up or sharing advice, and Skin Concerns (textarea) for users to describe specific issues such as redness, scarring, or frequent breakouts. Additional fields will include a Current Skincare Routine (checkbox list) to understand the products they already use, and Lifestyle Habits (multiple-choice) to assess factors like sleep, diet, and stress levels that might impact their acne. This form will enable users to receive individualized skincare tips.


Data for my table: My table, located on the "Skincare Products" page, will compare various products designed for acne-prone skin. The table will include columns for Product Name to identify each product, Price to help users gauge affordability, Key Ingredients to highlight acne-fighting components like salicylic acid or niacinamide, Benefits to describe specific outcomes such as reducing redness or preventing future breakouts, and Skin Type to indicate suitability for oily, dry, or combination skin. This table will provide an easy-to-read comparison to help users make informed decisions.

5. How I will collect information/images/content for my site. (2 references I plan on using)

I will gather information for my website through a combination of personal knowledge, research from reputable skincare and health websites, and input from trusted sources like dermatology experts and product reviews. For images, I will use free, non-copyrighted resources like Unsplash and Pexels, ensuring proper attribution, and may also create original graphics for unique content. To ensure credibility, I will include references from reliable sources such as skincare brand websites and health-focused articles.
