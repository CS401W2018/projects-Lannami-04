Foods that can worsen acne

High Glycemic Foods

Foods like white bread, sugary drinks, and processed snacks can spike blood sugar levels, leading to increased sebum production and clogged pores.

Dairy Products

Milk, cheese, and ice cream may promote the production of acne-causing hormones.

Fast Food

Diets rich in burgers, fries, and other fast food items are associated with higher acne risk due to their high fat and sugar content.

[Source: Healthline]

Foods that help in getting rid of acne

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Found in fish like salmon and sardines, omega-3s reduce inflammation and help manage acne.

Antioxidant-Rich Foods:

Berries, spinach, and green tea are high in antioxidants, which combat skin damage and reduce acne severity.

Probiotics:

Foods like yogurt, kefir, and fermented vegetables promote a healthy gut microbiome, which can positively affect skin health.

Vitamin A and Zinc-Rich Foods:

Sweet potatoes, carrots, and pumpkin provide vitamin A, while nuts and seeds supply zinc, both essential for clear skin.

[Source: Healthline]
