Eat Your Way to Healthy Skin

A healthy diet plays a crucial role in maintaining clear skin. Here are some tips to improve your skin through nutrition:

  • Eat More Fruits and Vegetables: These are rich in vitamins and antioxidants that combat acne.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking plenty of water flushes out toxins and keeps your skin hydrated.
  • Incorporate Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Found in fish, walnuts, and flaxseed, these reduce inflammation and acne.
  • Avoid Sugary Foods: High-sugar foods can trigger insulin spikes, leading to breakouts.
  • Cut Back on Dairy: Some studies suggest that dairy can exacerbate acne in some individuals.
