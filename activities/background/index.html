When should you use a background image, and when should you use the IMG tag?

When the image is part of the design, we use a background image. For example, we want to style the appearance of our page, then we use a background image. On the other hand, when an image is a component of the content of the page and has to be viewed and understood by users, such as product images, photos included in articles, or any other content that enhances the information on the page, we should then use the IMG tag.
