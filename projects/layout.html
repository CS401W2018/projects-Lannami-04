A children's tale that speaks volume as we grow

The story of The Little Prince takes readers on a journey through the eyes of a young boy who travels from planet to planet, meeting curious characters along the way. Each encounter teaches the prince, and the reader, profound lessons about life, love, and human nature. Through his observations, the little prince learns that the most important things in life are often invisible to the eye.

At the heart of the story is the relationship between the little prince and his rose, which represents love, beauty, and responsibility. Despite her thorns, the rose teaches him the complexities of caring for someone and the sacrifices love demands.
