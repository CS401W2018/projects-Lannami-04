About Men's Gymnastics

Men's gymnastics has been a part of the Olympic Games since 1896. We can see how the sport has evolved through the years by introducing various disciplines and techniques. Men's gymnastics involves 6 events: (1) Floor Exercise (2) Pommel Horse (3) Still Rings (4) Vault (5) Parallel Bars (6) Horizontal Bar. Some also compete in the individual all-around events which combines scores from all 6 disciplines.
